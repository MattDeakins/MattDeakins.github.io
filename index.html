Our Services

Whatever your product needs - Whether you need support ongoing, ad hoc, or for fixed-term projects, Firefly Labs works closely with you to craft bespoke solutions that fit your exact needs.

Leadership & operations

Fractional product leadership that works alongside your executive team to embed product thinking across your organisation.

Whether you’re looking to establish or refine operational structures, we help your team evolve and deliver additional value utilising product principles.

Strategy, discovery & design

Is your current product not delivering the results you expect, or do you need to realign your product with an evolving market?

We can help you redefine your strategy, strengthen your product proposition, improve your user experience, or tip the needle on specific business objectives.

Problem solving & delivery

Sometimes you need someone to step in to help solve issues, deliver solutions, or launch features.

When time or resources are tight, we can bridge the gap with experienced resource for hands-on support. Our delivery support provides a flexible contract resource embedded in your team.
